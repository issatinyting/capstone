Rumi: Queen of Secrecy, Song & Shadow

If you’ve ever swooned over a character who fights demons and also fights her own identity—you need to meet Rumi. She’s the lead vocalist and fierce leader of HUNTR/X, a K‑pop girl group whose glow onstage only hints at how heavy her life really is

  • Rumi is half-demon, which is her secret. Her mom was human (and a former demon hunter idol), and her father was a demon. Her mother died when Rumi was born; she was raised by Celine (a fellow hunter/idol) who tries to protect Rumi by making her hide her demon side
  • The biggest thing is that she’s constantly hiding part of herself — her demon markings, her voice strain, the fear that people (especially Mira & Zoey) will reject her if they find out.
  • At first it’s all about protecting her image, protecting her friends, protecting the Honmoon (this magical barrier she and her group uphold to keep demons at bay). She believes that if the Honmoon turns gold, it might “erase” her demonic marks. But through the story, she starts realizing that hiding isn’t healing. She confronts betrayal, rejection, shame—all the stuff we hide from in real life—and begins to accept the “demon” half of herself, realizing it’s part of her strength, not just something to be ashamed of.

Mira Huntrix — A Fan Girl’s Bio

Stage Role: Visual & Main Dancer of Huntr/x, the triple-threat idol-demon-slayer we all stan.

Backstory: Born into a strict chaebol family, Mira broke away to chase her passion — K-pop stardom and demon justice. That rebellious queen energy? Yes please.

Why She’s Everything: She's cool on the outside, loyal to the bone, and fights demons with the elegance of a runway model wielding a polearm. 💅

Signature Weapon: A woldo — it’s long, lethal, and matches her deadly grace on stage and in battle.

Fave Moment: That scene where she twirls mid-dance, slices a demon mid-beat, and doesn’t miss a single choreo step. Literal chills.
